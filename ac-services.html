Air Conditioning Services

At DTR Air Conditioning and Refrigeration Services, we are your trusted source for all your air conditioning needs. With years of experience and a commitment to customer satisfaction, we provide a wide range of air conditioning services to keep you cool and comfortable year-round.

Installation


When it comes to installing air conditioning systems, precision is key. Our team of certified technicians will expertly install your new AC unit, ensuring it operates efficiently and effectively. Whether you need a central air system, ductless mini-split, or window unit, we have the knowledge and experience to get the job done right.

Repairs


Is your AC unit not cooling properly, making strange noises, or experiencing other issues? Our skilled technicians are ready to diagnose and repair any air conditioning problems you may encounter. We use the latest techniques and quality replacement parts to ensure your AC runs smoothly.

Maintenance


Regular maintenance is essential to prolong the life and efficiency of your air conditioning system. Our maintenance plans include comprehensive inspections, cleaning, and tune-ups to keep your AC in optimal condition. Prevent costly breakdowns and enjoy consistent cooling with our maintenance services.

Upgrades and Replacements


If your current AC system is outdated or inefficient, it may be time for an upgrade or replacement. We offer a wide selection of energy-efficient air conditioning units that can save you money on your energy bills while providing superior cooling performance. Our experts will help you choose the right system for your needs and budget.

Contact Us


Whether you need AC installation, repairs, maintenance, or an upgrade, we are here to provide you with top-notch air conditioning services. Contact us today to schedule an appointment, request a quote, or discuss your cooling needs with our experts. Trust DTR Air Conditioning and Refrigeration Services to keep you cool and comfortable throughout the year.
