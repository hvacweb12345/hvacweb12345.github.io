HVAC Inspections

Welcome to Bickel Heating and Cooling's HVAC Inspections service, tailored to meet the unique needs of realtors and ensure seamless real estate transactions. Our inspections focus on verifying the functionality of the main components of HVAC systems, assuring both sellers and buyers that the home's heating and cooling systems are in optimal condition for a smooth transition in ownership.

Main Component Checks

Our inspections prioritize the examination of key HVAC components, including the furnace, air conditioner, ventilation systems, and thermostat. We ensure that each element is functioning as intended to maintain a comfortable living environment.


Performance Verification

Beyond visual inspections, we conduct performance tests to confirm the efficiency of the HVAC system. This includes evaluating heating and cooling capabilities, ensuring consistent airflow, and validating thermostat responsiveness.


Documentation for Peace of Mind

We provide detailed documentation of our inspections, offering realtors and their clients a comprehensive overview of the HVAC system's condition. This documentation serves as a valuable resource during real estate transactions.
