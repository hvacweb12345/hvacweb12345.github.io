Common HVAC Problems We Fix

HVAC systems can encounter a variety of issues, from minor inconveniences to major malfunctions. Some common problems we address include:

Refrigerant Leaks:
Low refrigerant levels can reduce cooling efficiency and cause your system to work harder, leading to higher energy bills and potential damage.

Thermostat Issues:
A malfunctioning thermostat can result in uneven temperatures and discomfort in your space.

Dirty or Clogged Filters:
Dirty filters restrict airflow, reducing system efficiency and indoor air quality.

Electrical Problems:
Faulty wiring or electrical components can cause your system to short cycle or not turn on at all.

Mechanical Wear and Tear:
Over time, various components of your HVAC system can wear out, leading to reduced performance or complete breakdowns.
